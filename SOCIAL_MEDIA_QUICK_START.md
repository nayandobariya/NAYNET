## ğŸš€ Getting Started

### Step 1: Access the Social Network
1. **Login** to your account (employee or employer)
2. Look for **"Social Network"** in the main navigation menu
3. Click it to enter the social media section

### Step 2: Create Your First Post
1. On the **Posts** page, you'll see a "Create Post" form at the top
2. Type your message in the text area
3. **(Optional)** Click the camera icon to add an image
4. Click **"Share Post"** to publish

### Step 3: Interact with Posts
- **Like**: Click the thumbs-up icon on any post
- **Comment**: Click the comment icon and type your comment
- **View Comments**: Click "Comment" to expand the comments section

### Step 4: Connect with Others
1. Click **"My Network"** in the social header
2. Browse **Connection Suggestions**
3. Click **"Follow"** to follow someone
4. Send **Connection Requests** to build your network

### Step 5: Start Chatting
1. Click **"Messages"** in the social header
2. Click **"New Message"** button
3. Search for a user
4. Start typing your message
5. Press Enter or click send
